About me

Hi, I'm God's Bright, a web developer and graphic designer based in Nigeria. With a passion for creating seamless user experiences, I strive to bridge the gap between design and technology.

Background

I graduated from ISMT St Salomon University with a degree in Computer Science. Over the years, I have gained extensive experience in the tech industry, working for Non Governmental Organizations.

Expertise and Skills

With a strong foundation in front-end development, I specialize in building responsive and accessible websites. My expertise includes:

  • HTML/CSS: Crafting pixel-perfect layouts that are both beautiful and functional.
  • JavaScript: Developing dynamic and interactive web applications.
  • React: Leveraging the power of modern frameworks to create scalable solutions.

Personal Touch

I believe in continuous learning and innovation, which drives me to stay updated with the latest trends in web development. When I'm not coding, you can find me hiking in the mountains or enjoying a good book at a local café, always looking for new experiences and opportunities to grow.
